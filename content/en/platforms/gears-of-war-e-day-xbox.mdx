<Section icon={Target} title="Campaign Story, Timeline, and Co-Op Setup">

The campaign of this prequel is set fourteen years before the events of the original Gears of War. It covers the immediate aftermath of the Pendulum Wars, shortly after peace is declared on Sera. The narrative focuses on a compact, high-intensity timeline spanning just three days in the city of Kalona.

Rather than focusing on a sprawling global conflict, the story is deeply personal. It follows young veterans Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago as they navigate the sudden, terrifying emergence of the Locust Horde. Both characters are carrying the emotional weight of losing Carlos Santiago, Dom's brother and Marcus's best friend, which adds immediate tension to their partnership.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Weapons, Traversal, and Combat Mechanics">

Combat is designed around a modernized version of the classic cover-shooter puzzle. While iconic weapons return, players must also master new tools and environmental hazards. Traversal has been completely rebuilt to introduce verticality, allowing players to jump across rooftops, scale balconies, and find new flanking routes.

The arsenal features a mix of classic designs, early prototypes, and battlefield modifications. Notably, the campaign explores the origin of the Chainsaw Lancer, which is kit-bashed on the front lines out of sheer desperation.

| Weapon | Category | Combat Role | Optimal Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|
| Gnasher | Returning | Close-range shotgun | Push tight spaces, control cover corners |
| Longshot | Returning | Precision rifle | Hold elevated positions, support ground squads |
| Gut Puncher | New | Explosive launcher | Fire armor-piercing rounds behind enemy cover |
| Incinerator | New | Imulsion shotgun | Deploy high-damage fire to halt aggressive rushes |
| Chainsaw Lancer | Origin | Hybrid rifle | Mid-range suppressive fire, close-range execution |

Environmental destruction also plays a major role in combat. Micro-destruction chips away at stone and wooden cover, making defensive positions temporary. On a larger scale, massive sinkholes can open up mid-battle, swallowing vehicles and altering the layout of the map in real time.

<Callout type="tip" title="E-Hole Management">
Do not ignore active E-Holes. A single well-placed grenade from a Breacher or a quick squad push can seal the hole before high-tier Locust units overrun your position.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="PC System Requirements and Console Performance">

To experience the visual leap offered by Unreal Engine 5, players must ensure their hardware meets the modern performance baselines. The game utilizes advanced software-based and hardware-accelerated ray tracing, requiring a solid-state drive (SSD) for both Xbox and PC platforms to handle asset streaming without hitching.

The console versions are optimized specifically for the Xbox architecture. Xbox Series X targets a native 4K resolution at 60 FPS, utilizing spatial audio technologies like Dolby Atmos and DTS:X to deliver an immersive, atmospheric horror experience.

| Specification | Minimum PC Target | Recommended PC Target |
|---|---|---|
| OS | Windows 10 64-bit (22H2) | Windows 11 64-bit (25H2) |
| Processor | AMD Ryzen 5 2600X / Intel i5-10400 | AMD Ryzen 5 5600 / Intel i5-11600K |
| Memory | 12 GB RAM | 16 GB RAM |
| Graphics | RTX 2060 / RX 6600 / Arc A580 | RTX 3060 Ti / RX 6700 XT / Arc B580 |
| DirectX | Version 12 | Version 12 |
| Storage | 130 GB available space (SSD) | 130 GB available space (SSD) |

For PC players, a high-speed broadband internet connection is required for online multiplayer, and Steam players must link their profiles to a free Xbox Live account to access cross-play matchmaking.

<Callout type="warning" title="SSD Requirement">
Traditional mechanical hard drives (HDDs) are not supported. Installing the game on an HDD will result in severe texture pop-in, audio desynchronization, and loading failures.
</Callout>
