Setting up your **Gears of War E-Day crossplay** and cross-save environment is straightforward. Because the game relies on the Xbox network, your identity is tied to your Gamertag. This means your achievements in the campaign and your unlocks in the multiplayer modes are globally accessible.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Sign in to Xbox Live">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Enable Cloud Saves">
    Ensure that 'Cloud Saves' are enabled in your console settings or within the Xbox App on PC. This is usually active by default.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Link External Accounts">
    If playing on Steam, go to the in-game settings and link your Steam account to your Xbox Live account.

  <Step num="4" title="Verify Sync">

  </Step>
