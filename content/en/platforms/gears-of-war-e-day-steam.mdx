<Section icon={Shield} title="PC System Requirements and Hardware Targets">

To experience the destruction of Kalona in full detail, PC players need to ensure their hardware meets the modern demands of Unreal Engine 5. The game utilizes advanced rendering techniques, including Ray Tracing and micro-destruction, which require modern GPUs and high-speed storage. A solid-state drive (SSD) is mandatory for both minimum and recommended configurations to handle real-time asset streaming without stuttering.

Below are the official PC system requirements confirmed for the Steam and Windows PC versions.

| Hardware Component | Minimum Requirement | Recommended Specification |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Operating System** | Windows 10 64-bit (v22H2 19045.7291) | Windows 11 64-bit (v25H2 or newer) |
| **Processor (AMD)** | AMD Ryzen 5 2600X | AMD Ryzen 5 5600 |
| **Processor (Intel)** | Intel Core i7-6850K / i5-10400 | Intel Core i5-11600K |
| **Memory (RAM)** | 12 GB RAM | 16 GB RAM |
| **Graphics (NVIDIA)** | GeForce RTX 5050 / RTX 2060 | GeForce RTX 5060 / RTX 3060 Ti |
| **Graphics (AMD)** | Radeon RX 6600 / RX 9060 | Radeon RX 6700 XT / RX 9060 XT |
| **Graphics (Intel)** | Intel Arc A580 | Intel Arc B580 |
| **DirectX Version** | Version 12 (Feature Level 12_0) | Version 12 (Feature Level 12_0) |
| **Storage Space** | 130 GB available space | 130 GB available space |
| **Storage Type** | SSD Required | SSD Required |

The minimum configuration targets stable performance at lower resolutions, utilizing modern entry-level GPUs like the RTX 5050 or older mainstays like the RTX 2060. For players looking to enable high-fidelity settings, Ray Tracing, and high frame rates, the recommended specifications point toward mid-range hardware such as the RTX 5060 or the RX 6700 XT.

<Callout type="tip" title="Storage and Driver Preparation">
Ensure you have at least 130 GB of free space on an internal SSD. Installing the game on a traditional mechanical Hard Disk Drive (HDD) will result in severe loading bottlenecks, audio desynchronization, and asset pop-in during heavy combat sequences.
</Callout>
