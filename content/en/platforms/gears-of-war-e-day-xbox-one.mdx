<Section icon={Target} title="How to Play Gears of War: E-Day on Xbox One">

If you want to experience the origin story of Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago on your legacy console, you must use **Xbox Cloud Gaming**. This service streams the game directly from custom Xbox Series X server blades to your local screen, requiring no heavy rendering from your legacy console.

Follow this step-by-step setup guide to prepare your legacy console for the October 6, 2026 launch.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Subscribe to Xbox Game Pass Ultimate">
    Xbox Cloud Gaming is an exclusive feature of the Xbox Game Pass Ultimate tier. Ensure your subscription is active prior to the open beta or launch day.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Optimize Your Network Connection">
    Cloud streaming requires a highly stable internet connection. Connect your Xbox One directly to your router using an Ethernet cable, or use a high-speed 5GHz Wi-Fi band. A minimum download speed of 20 Mbps is recommended.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Locate the Game on the Dashboard">
    On launch day, open the Xbox Game Pass library on your Xbox One dashboard. Search for Gears of War: E-Day and look for the cloud icon on the game's tile.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Launch and Stream Instantly">
    Select the game and click the "Play" button with the cloud icon. The game will launch instantly without requiring a massive 130 GB local installation on your hard drive.
  </Step>
</Steps>
