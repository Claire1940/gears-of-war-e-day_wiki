The campaign serves as a personal survival story set fourteen years before the events of the original Gears of War. Over the course of three brutal days, players will witness the collapse of the city of Kalona as the Locust Horde emerges from the depths of Sera.

### The Three-Day Timeline

The narrative is structured tightly around the immediate aftermath of Emergence Day, focusing on panic, confusion, and desperate defense.

- **Phase 1 (Day One - First Contact)**: Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago are caught in the initial outbreak in Kalona. Without proper Intel, they must survive encounters with terrifying, unnamed subterranean creatures.
- **Phase 2 (Day Two - Bravo Squad Forms)**: Together with Mags Carter (an Imulsion refinery worker) and Lucas Reyes (a young cadet), Marcus and Dom form Bravo Squad to establish a defensive line and rescue stranded civilians.
- **Phase 3 (Day Three - The Fall of Kalona)**: As organized military communication breaks down, Bravo Squad must make a final stand against an escalating Locust force, realizing that this is not an isolated riot, but a world-ending war.
