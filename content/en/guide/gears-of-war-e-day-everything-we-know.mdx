<Section icon={Target} title="Campaign Story, Timeline, and Bravo Squad">

Set fourteen years before the events of the original Gears of War, E-Day strips away the massive military infrastructure of the later Coalition of Ordered Governments (COG) wars. Instead, it presents a raw, claustrophobic look at a society completely unprepared for an underground invasion. The entire campaign takes place in a single city, Kalona, spanning three intense days of societal collapse.

The narrative centers on a younger Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago, both dealing with the recent loss of Carlos Santiago (Dom's older brother and Marcus's best friend). This shared grief forms the emotional backbone of their partnership as they transition from war-weary veterans to desperate survivors.

| Phase | Timeframe | Story Focus | Key Characters |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Before Emergence** | Post-Pendulum Wars | Emotional setup and grief | Marcus, Dom, Carlos (grief) |
| **Day One** | Emergence Day | First Locust contact in Kalona | Marcus, Dom |
| **Bravo Squad** | Early Chaos | Squad formation and refinery | Marcus, Dom, Mags, Lucas |
| **Day Two** | Regrouping | Military response across districts | Bravo Squad, Kalona defenders |
| **Day Three** | Fall of Kalona | Escalating threat and full war | Bravo Squad, Locust Horde, Tai |

The campaign is built to support multiple playstyles, offering a flexible cooperative environment for friends looking to experience the origin story together.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Multiplayer Evolution: Horde Siege and Versus PvP">

Multiplayer returns with a massive structural upgrade. The headline addition is **Horde Siege**, an evolved PvE wave-survival mode that expands the classic 5-player cooperative format into a chaotic 12-player battlefield. Players are divided into three distinct 4-player squads, working together to defend sprawling sectors of Kalona from escalating Locust waves and massive world bosses.

| Class | Role | Core Focus | Strategy Tip |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Assault** | Frontline Combat | High damage, close-quarters | Push objectives with Gnasher |
| **Marksman** | Precision Support | Long-range sniping, sightlines | Hold high ground with Longshot |
| **Medic** | Squad Survival | Healing, reviving, defense | Stay near cover, support Breachers |
| **Breacher** | Heavy Demolition | Armor-piercing, structural damage | Use Gut Puncher on E-Holes |

For competitive players, **Versus PvP** returns with refined 4v4 tactical combat. The multiplayer maps are built specifically around Kalona's urban environments, featuring symmetrical designs that reward map control, flanking, and vertical positioning. Both Ranked and Social playlists will be available at launch, offering tailored environments for competitive and casual players alike.

<Callout type="warning" title="Multiplayer Requirements">
Online multiplayer and cooperative modes, including Horde Siege and Versus PvP, require an active Game Pass Core or Ultimate subscription on Xbox consoles. PC players do not require a subscription to play online via Steam or the Xbox App.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Weapons, Traversal, and Combat Mechanics">

Combat in Gears of War: E-Day is built around a modernized version of the classic cover-shooter puzzle. Rebuilt in Unreal Engine 5, the game introduces micro-destruction, allowing bullets and explosives to chip away at defensive positions. Cover is no longer permanent, forcing players to constantly reposition as environments crumble.

Iconic weapons return alongside brutal prototype tools developed on the front lines of the invasion.

| Weapon | Category | Combat Role | Best Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Gnasher** | Returning | Close-range shotgun | Close-quarters cover fights |
| **Longshot** | Returning | Precision sniper rifle | Rooftop flanking and sightlines |
| **Gut Puncher** | New | Explosive grenade launcher | Detonating behind enemy cover |
| **Incinerator** | New | Imulsion-fueled shotgun | High-pressure crowd control |
| **Chainsaw Lancer** | Origin | Battlefield-built signature | Brutal melee executions |

Traversal has been completely overhauled to introduce more fluid movement options. For the first time in the series, players can jump across gaps, climb vertical obstacles, and utilize elevated flanking routes. Sliding from a sprint allows players to dive under low-hanging objects, slide around corners, and quickly transition into low-ground cover.

<Callout type="tip" title="Tactical Advice">
Keep an eye out for emerging E-Holes. When the ground opens, use the Gut Puncher's player-detonated explosives or standard frag grenades to seal the hole immediately before advanced Locust forces can overwhelm your squad.
</Callout>

</Section>
