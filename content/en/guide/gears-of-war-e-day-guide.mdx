<Section icon={Users} title="Campaign Story, Timeline, and Co-op Setup">

The narrative of Gears of War: E-Day is a personal survival story rather than a sweeping military campaign. It takes place entirely within the city of Kalona over a claustrophobic three-day period. Players control a younger, battle-weary Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago as they navigate the immediate aftermath of the Locust emergence.

The emotional core of the story centers on the recent loss of Carlos Santiago, Dom’s older brother and Marcus's best friend. This tragedy shapes their early relationship, making their cooperation a matter of survival rather than established brotherhood.

| Character | Role in Bravo Squad | Background |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Marcus Fenix** | Frontline Soldier | Pendulum Wars veteran carrying deep emotional scars |
| **Dominic Santiago** | Support Combatant | Searching for purpose after the loss of his brother Carlos |
| **Mags Carter** | Heavy Specialist | Former Gear working at Kalona Imulsion Refinery |
| **Lucas Reyes** | Communications Officer | Young cadet who has never experienced frontline combat |

The campaign is playable solo or with friends. Console players can enjoy classic two-player split-screen co-op, while online play supports up to four players. Players can select from any of the four Bravo Squad members during online co-op sessions.

<Callout type="info" title="Campaign Structure">
The campaign does not feature open-world segments. It is a highly focused, linear story designed to emphasize the horror and confusion of the first three days of the invasion.

<Section icon={Swords} title="Gears of War E-Day Guide: Beginner Combat and Traversal">

This **Gears of War E-Day guide** highlights how the classic combat puzzle has been modernized. The core gameplay still relies heavily on taking cover, but traversal has been rebuilt to offer unprecedented tactical freedom.

For the first time in the series, players can jump across gaps, climb vertical routes, and execute fluid cover transitions. This changes how you approach flanking maneuvers and high-ground defense.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master the Slide and Cover Transitions">
    Practice sliding from a sprint into low-ground or high-ground cover. Cover is destructible, so you must constantly transition to new walls as enemy fire chips away at your protection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Vertical Flanks">
    Look for balconies, rooftops, and fire escapes. Use the new jump mechanic to reach elevated positions, allowing you to rain precision fire down on Locust forces with the Longshot rifle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Plug Emergence Holes Immediately">
    When the ground opens, react fast. E-Holes can quickly overrun your squad with Wretches and Drones. Toss a well-aimed frag grenade or fire an explosive round into the opening to seal it.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Your Weapon Ranges">
    Equip a balanced loadout. Keep the Gnasher close for aggressive rushers, and use the new Gut Puncher or Incinerator shotguns to control tight corridors and choke points.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The arsenal features a mix of returning classics and prototype weapons engineered during the first days of the war.

| Weapon | Category | Combat Role | Best Tactical Use |

<Section icon={Trophy} title="Horde Siege PvE Classes and Versus PvP Map Tactics">

Multiplayer in Gears of War: E-Day is split into two primary experiences: the expanded 12-player Horde Siege PvE mode and the highly competitive 4v4 Versus PvP mode. Both modes leverage the game's updated traversal and destruction mechanics.

Horde Siege is a massive cooperative battle where three distinct 4-player squads defend Kalona against escalating waves of the Locust Horde. To succeed, players must select complementary classes and coordinate their defenses.

| Class | Combat Archetype | Unique Ability | Preferred Weaponry |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Assault** | Frontline Combatant | Increased damage on consecutive hits | Gnasher, Lancer |
| **Marksman** | Long-Range Support | Critical hits reveal adjacent enemies | Longshot, Pistol |
| **Medic** | Squad Support | Rapid revive and health regeneration field | Lancer, Submachine Gun |
| **Breacher** | Close-Quarter Specialist | Shield boost when sliding into cover | Gut Puncher, Incinerator |

Versus PvP returns to a tighter, more tactical 4v4 structure. Symmetrical map layouts are set in war-torn districts of Kalona, such as the sports stadium, industrial waterfront, and the refinery zones. Winning matches requires map control, weapon spawn timing, and mastering the modernized slide mechanics.

| **RAM** | 12 GB | 16 GB |
| **GPU** | RTX 2060 / RX 6600 / Arc A580 | RTX 3060 Ti / RX 6700 XT / Arc B580 |
| **DirectX** | Version 12 | Version 12 |
| **Storage** | 130 GB available space (SSD required) | 130 GB available space (SSD required) |

Xbox Series X|S players benefit from targeted optimizations. The game runs at a target 4K resolution at up to 60 frames per second across both the Campaign and Multiplayer modes on compatible displays. Advanced console features include Ray Tracing, HDR10, Variable Refresh Rate (VRR), and Dolby Atmos spatial audio.

<Callout type="warning" title="SSD Storage Mandatory">
Mechanical hard drives (HDDs) are not supported. Installing the game on an HDD will result in severe texture pop-in, loading delays, and audio desynchronization.
