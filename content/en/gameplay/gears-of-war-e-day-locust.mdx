<Section icon={Target} title="Tactical Combat Mechanics Against the Horde">

Fighting the underground threat requires mastering both classic cover-based shooting and modernized traversal mechanics. For the first time, players can jump across rooftops, climb vertical pathways, and slide fluidly around corners. This added mobility is essential because the environment itself is destructible; micro-destruction chips away at stone and metal cover, making defensive positions temporary.

When Emergence Holes (E-Holes) open, they present an immediate tactical crisis that must be resolved before the squad is overwhelmed by endless reinforcements.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Identify the Spawn Site">
    Watch for ground cracks, heavy dust clouds, and screen shaking, which indicate an active E-Hole is opening nearby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Clear Immediate Spawns">
    Deploy close-range weapons like the Gnasher or the Imulsion-fueled Incinerator to neutralize the first wave of emerging enemies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Secure High Ground">
    Use the new jump and vertical traversal mechanics to climb balconies or rooftops, establishing clear sightlines over the spawn point.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Seal the E-Hole">
    Throw a well-placed frag grenade or fire an explosive round from the Gut Puncher directly into the glowing center of the hole to close it permanently.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Vertical Traversal">
Use vertical flanks to shoot down behind Locust cover. Elevated positions provide a massive tactical advantage against advancing Drones.
</Callout>

</Section>
