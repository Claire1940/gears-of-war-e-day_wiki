<Section icon={Map} title="Gears of War E-Day Split Screen Setup">

Setting up local play requires proper controller synchronization and profile assignment. The console version supports a seamless drop-in, drop-out system, allowing a second player to join the action without losing campaign progress.

To ensure a smooth setup process, follow these steps to configure your second controller and sign in correctly.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Connect Controllers">
    Turn on the second Xbox controller and sync it with your Xbox Series console.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Assign Profiles">
    Sign the second controller into a separate Xbox profile or select a guest account.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Launch Campaign">
    Start the main campaign from the host profile and enter the gameplay lobby.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Join Game">
    Press the Menu button on the second controller to join the active lobby session.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Profile Saving">
Using a registered secondary profile instead of a guest account ensures that collectible progress and achievements are saved for both players.
</Callout>
