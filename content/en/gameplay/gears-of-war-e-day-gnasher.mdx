This allows you to close the distance against entrenched Locust Drones without exposing your hitbox to devastating mid-range rifle fire.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Scenarios and Enemy Countermeasures">

Sera's downfall begins with chaos, and the Locust Horde utilizes terrifying tactics to overwhelm Coalition forces. Emergence Holes (E-Holes) can open dynamically beneath your feet, dumping Drones, Wretches, and massive subterranean beasts directly onto the battlefield. When an E-Hole opens, the Gnasher is your best asset for holding the line while a squadmate prepares an explosive to seal the breach.

Additionally, Gears of War: E-Day introduces micro-destruction mechanics. Concrete barriers, wooden crates, and metal sheets will chip away under sustained gunfire. This means your cover is never permanent, and you must constantly reposition using your slide mechanics to avoid being exposed to lethal crossfire.

<Callout type="warning" title="E-Hole Danger">
