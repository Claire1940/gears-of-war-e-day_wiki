<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Guide to Pre-Ordering and Claiming Your Content">

Securing your copy of the game and ensuring you receive your physical and digital bonuses requires following the correct purchase and redemption steps. Because physical editions use different distribution methods than digital storefronts, pay close attention to how your retailer handles beta codes and launch day deliveries.

To buy the game, you can visit the [official Xbox store page](https://www.xbox.com/en-US/games/gears-of-war-eday) or check the [Steam store page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3010850/Gears_of_War_EDay/) for PC pre-purchases. For physical bundles, check with major gaming retailers in your region.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Preferred Retailer">
    Choose an authorized retailer for physical purchases to guarantee you receive an authentic package. For digital copies, purchase directly through the Microsoft Store, Xbox console dashboard, or Steam.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Retrieve Your Open Beta Early Access Code">
    If you pre-ordered digitally, your account is automatically flagged for the August 6, 2026 Open Beta. If you purchased a physical edition, check your receipt or email inbox for a single-use 25-character beta code.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Redeem the Code via Xbox Live">
    Log into your primary Xbox profile on your console or Windows PC. Navigate to the store tab, select 'Redeem Code', and enter the digits provided by your retailer to unlock your beta client download.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unbox and Claim Launch Day Rewards">
    Upon receiving your physical package, locate the digital insert card inside the custom display packaging. Redeem this code to apply the Premium Edition digital upgrade, including the Exfil Dom skin and 1,000 Iron, to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Retailer Verification Tip">
Always verify that your physical retailer is an official participant in the pre-order bonus program. Some regional stores may not distribute the Exfil skin pack or the Open Beta codes automatically, requiring you to contact customer support with proof of purchase.
</Callout>

For players using Steam, a linked third-party Xbox Live account is required to access multiplayer features, cross-play, and to sync your digital pre-order entitlements across platforms.
