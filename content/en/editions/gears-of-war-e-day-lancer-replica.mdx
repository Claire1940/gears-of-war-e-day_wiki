<Section icon={Shield} title="Displaying and Maintaining Your Replica Collection">

Owning a 1:1 scale prop replica comes with the responsibility of proper maintenance. Because these items are primarily constructed from ABS plastic and contain delicate electronics for light and sound features, they must be stored in a controlled environment.

Direct sunlight is the primary enemy of plastic collectibles. Ultraviolet rays can break down the chemical bonds in ABS plastic, leading to paint fading, yellowing, and brittleness over time. Display your replica in a room with controlled lighting, or use UV-resistant acrylic display cases.
