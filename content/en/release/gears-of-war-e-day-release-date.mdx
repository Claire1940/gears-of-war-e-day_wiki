<Section icon={Swords} title="Campaign Co-Op, Characters, and Story Timeline">

Set fourteen years before the original *Gears of War*, the campaign of *E-Day* centers on the immediate, terrifying chaos of Emergence Day in the city of Kalona. Rather than a massive military operation, the story is framed as a personal fight for survival over a condensed three-day timeline.

Young veterans Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago return from the Pendulum Wars wounded by the loss of Carlos Santiago—Dom's brother and Marcus's best friend. When the Locust Horde erupts from beneath the city, they must put aside their grief to lead Bravo Squad through the breaking streets.

| Phase | Timeline Focus | Key Characters | Urban Environments |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pre-Emergence** | Armistice memorial events | Marcus, Dom | Central Plaza, Memorial Parks |
| **Day One** | First contact and confusion | Marcus, Dom | Imulsion Refinery, Residential Districts |
| **Day Two** | Military regrouping & defense | Mags Carter, Lucas Reyes | Sports Stadium, Airfield |
| **Day Three** | Full urban collapse | Bravo Squad, Tai Kaliso | Industrial Waterfront, Sinkholes |

The campaign supports multiple cooperative configurations, allowing players to experience this origin story together. Console players can utilize classic two-player split-screen co-op, while online play supports up to four players. The playable roster of Bravo Squad includes Marcus, Dom, refinery worker Mags Carter, and communications cadet Lucas Reyes.

<Callout type="info" title="A Return to Survival Horror">
Unlike the high-tech military tone of later sequels, *E-Day* emphasizes the raw terror of first contact. The Locust are presented as overwhelming, nightmarish monsters that require tactical cover, environmental awareness, and coordinated squad movement to defeat.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Multiplayer Modes: Horde Siege and Versus PvP">

Multiplayer returns with a focus on tactical squad play, modernized traversal, and vertical map design. Players can participate in competitive 4v4 Versus matches or join forces in the expanded cooperative Horde Siege mode.

### Horde Siege PvE
Horde Siege expands the classic wave-survival formula into a 12-player cooperative battlefield. Three squads of four players must coordinate across large-scale city maps to defend defensive points, complete shared field objectives, and defeat massive world bosses emerging from underground sinkholes.

| Class | Combat Role | Primary Focus | Starting Loadout |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Assault** | Frontline Combat | High-mobility flanking and close-range damage | Chainsaw Lancer, Snub Pistol |
| **Marksman** | Long-Range Support | Precision weakpoint damage from elevated cover | Longshot Rifle, Snub Pistol |
| **Medic** | Squad Survival | Area-of-effect healing and remote revives | Lancer, Medical Stim |
| **Breacher** | Defensive Control | Heavy armor destruction and close-quarters defense | Gnasher Shotgun, Incinerator |

### Versus PvP
Competitive multiplayer focuses on tight, intense 4v4 combat across symmetrical maps set within the war-torn districts of Kalona. Traversal mechanics have been rebuilt to support sliding, low-ground cover transitions, and vertical jumps, allowing players to flank enemies from balconies and rooftops. Ranked and Social playlists will be available at launch, featuring classic game modes alongside new objective-based variants.

<Callout type="success" title="Co-Op Synergy is Key">
In Horde Siege, combining different class abilities is vital for surviving later waves. For example, a Breacher can shatter a boss's heavy armor with an explosive Gut Puncher round, leaving a vulnerable weakpoint for a Marksman to exploit with a high-damage Longshot rifle.
</Callout>

Built from the ground up on Unreal Engine 5, the game delivers advanced visual features including hardware-accelerated Ray Tracing, real-time micro-destruction, and dynamic lighting. PC players will require modern hardware and a mandatory solid-state drive (SSD) to run the game smoothly.

| **Memory (RAM)** | 12 GB RAM | 16 GB RAM |
| **Graphics (GPU)** | RTX 5050 / RTX 2060 / RX 6600 | RTX 5060 / RTX 3060 Ti / RX 6700 XT |
| **DirectX Version** | Version 12 | Version 12 |
| **Storage Space** | 130 GB available space | 130 GB available space |
| **Storage Type** | SSD Required | SSD Required |

Xbox Series X|S players will benefit from tailored optimizations, targeting 4K resolution and up to 60 FPS performance modes across both the campaign and multiplayer suites. Supported console features include HDR10, Variable Refresh Rate (VRR), and Dolby Atmos spatial audio.

<Callout type="warning" title="SSD Storage Mandatory">
Mechanical hard drives (HDDs) are not supported. Due to the high-bandwidth streaming requirements of Unreal Engine 5's virtualized geometry and textures, installing the game on an HDD will cause severe stuttering and asset loading failures.
</Callout>
