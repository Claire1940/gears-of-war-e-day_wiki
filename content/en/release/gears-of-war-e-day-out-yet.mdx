<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics, Traversal, and Rebuilt Weapons">

Gears of War E-Day acts as a prequel set fourteen years before the original game. The story covers the first three days of the Locust invasion in the city of Kalona. The Coalition is using this setting to strip away the grand military scale of later games, returning to the gritty, claustrophobic atmosphere of survival and horror.

To complement this tone, the core combat loop has been modernized with new movement mechanics, environmental destruction, and specialized weaponry.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Modernized Movement & Verticality">
    - **Smooth Cover Transitions**: Fluidly slide into cover across varying heights.
    - **Tactical Jumping**: Jump across rooftops, balconies, and broken structures.
    - **Vertical Flanks**: Attack enemies from elevated platforms and fire down into E-Holes.
    - **Combat Slides**: Slide from a sprint around corners to escape enemy crossfire.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Locust Threat & Dynamic Environments">
    - **E-Holes**: Locust emerge dynamically from the ground, requiring swift grenade closures.
    - **Sinkholes**: Large-scale environmental collapses pull down vehicles and structures.
    - **Micro-Destruction**: Continuous gunfire chips away stone, wood, and metal cover.
    - **Horror-Focused Enemies**: Reimagined Wretches and Drones feel like dangerous, terrifying monsters.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

The combat sandbox introduces a mix of iconic fan favorites and prototype weapons cobbled together during the chaos of Emergence Day.

- **Chainsaw Lancer Origin**: Witness the battlefield birth of the series' signature weapon, kit-bashed from a front-line prototype to combat the thick hides of the Locust.
- **Gnasher**: The close-range shotgun returns with its classic weight and stopping power, serving as the anchor for competitive multiplayer.
- **Longshot**: A high-caliber sniper rifle that rewards precision. It is perfect for holding down vertical sightlines from balconies and rooftops.
- **Gut Puncher**: A new armor-piercing grenade launcher. Players can fire rounds normally or hold the trigger to delay the explosion, flushing enemies out of cover.
- **Incinerator**: An Imulsion-fueled close-quarters shotgun designed to melt defensive positions and halt aggressive enemy rushes.

<Callout type="info" title="Horde Siege and Versus Modes">
Multiplayer features the traditional 4v4 Versus mode with Ranked and Social playlists, alongside an expanded **12-player Horde Siege PvE** mode. In Horde Siege, three squads of four players work together to defend Kalona across large maps, utilizing distinct classes like Assault, Marksman, Medic, and Breacher.
</Callout>
