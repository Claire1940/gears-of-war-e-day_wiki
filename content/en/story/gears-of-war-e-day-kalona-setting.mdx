<Section icon={Shield} title="Lore and History: The First City to Fall">

Kalona was not just any city; it was one of the first major urban centers to fall during the initial six weeks of the Locust War. The **gears of war e-day kalona setting** captures the sheer shock of a society that thought it had finally found peace after the Pendulum Wars.

**Historical Milestones:**
- **Post-Pendulum Wars**: Kalona was thriving as an industrial and cultural hub for Tyrus.
- **The Emergence**: The Locust Horde utilized the city's extensive subterranean infrastructure to strike multiple districts at once.
- **The Resistance**: Heroes like Marcus Fenix and Dominic Santiago fought alongside veterans like Mags Carter to hold back the tide.

The city's layout, featuring the Allfathers Legacy Bridge and the Octus Tribunal, highlights the COG's architectural dominance, which makes its rapid destruction all the more impactful.
