<Section icon={Swords} title="Gears of War E-Day Dom Santiago: Story, Timeline, and Campaign Role">

Dominic Santiago is a fundamental character in the Gears franchise, serving as the emotional anchor for Marcus Fenix throughout the original trilogy. In the prequel, the narrative shifts back to the very beginning of the Locust invasion. This story is set fourteen years before the events of the first game, focusing on a younger, raw version of Dom as he faces the collapse of his world.

The campaign takes place over three critical days in the city of Kalona. Marcus and Dom are in the city for an armistice memorial when the ground opens up and the Locust emerge. This is not a distant military briefing; it is a personal survival story. The transition from peace to a brutal, unknown conflict forces both characters to rely on instincts forged in previous battles.

Their relationship at the start of the game is wounded and distant. Both Marcus and Dom are carrying the trauma of losing Carlos Santiago, Dom’s older brother and Marcus’s best friend, during the Pendulum Wars. Working together during the emergence forces them to rebuild their bond under extreme pressure, laying the foundation for the brotherhood that defines the series.

<Callout type="info" title="Narrative Background">
The campaign of **gears of war e-day dom santiago** deals with the psychological aftermath of the Pendulum Wars. Carlos Santiago's death serves as a silent point of tension between Marcus and Dom when the Locust Horde first breaches the surface.
</Callout>

Instead of focusing on global military movements, the narrative scales down to focus on personal loyalty. As they witness the destruction of Kalona, Marcus and Dom must navigate the first-contact horror of enemies they do not yet understand.

Together, these characters provide the framework for the cooperative campaign.
